Waffles

Crispy, golden waffles perfect for breakfast or brunch.

Prep Time: 10 minutes
Cook Time: 15 minutes
Serves: 4-5 people

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 3/4 cups milk
  • 1/2 cup melted butter
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Mix dry ingredients: In a large bowl, whisk together flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  2. Combine wet ingredients: In another bowl, beat eggs, then add milk, melted butter, and vanilla.
  3. Make batter: Pour wet ingredients into dry ingredients. Stir until just combined (lumps are okay).
  4. Cook waffles: Preheat waffle iron. Pour batter onto hot waffle iron and cook according to manufacturer's instructions (usually 3-5 minutes).
  5. Serve: Serve immediately with butter, syrup, or your favorite toppings.
