Chicken Tikka

A flavorful grilled chicken dish marinated in yogurt and aromatic spices. Perfect as an appetizer or main course.

Prep Time: 15 minutes (plus marinating time)
Cook Time: 15 minutes
Serves: 4-6 people

Ingredients

For the Marinade:
  • 2 lbs boneless chicken (cut into 2-inch cubes)
  • 1 cup plain Greek yogurt
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 inch fresh ginger, grated
  • 2 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp ground coriander
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • 1/2 tsp cayenne pepper (adjust to taste)
  • 1 tsp salt
For Serving:
  • Sliced red onions
  • Fresh cilantro
  • Lemon wedges
  • Mint chutney (optional)

Instructions

  1. Prepare the marinade: Mix yogurt, lemon juice, oil, garlic, ginger, and all spices in a large bowl.
  2. Marinate the Chicken: Add chicken cubes to marinade, ensuring all pieces are well coated. Cover and refrigerate for at least 2 hours (preferably overnight).
  3. Prepare for Cooking: Remove chicken from fridge 30 minutes before cooking. Thread onto metal skewers if grilling.
  4. Cook the chicken
    • Grill: Cook over medium-high heat for 12-15 minutes, turning every 3-4 minutes
    • Oven: Bake at 450°F for 15-20 minutes on a lined baking sheet
  5. Check doneness: Internal temperature should reach 165°F and juices run clear.
  6. ServeGarnish with fresh cilantro, serve with sliced onions and lemon wedges.
