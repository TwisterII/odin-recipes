Lasagna

A hearty, layered pasta dish with rich meat sauce and creamy cheese.

Prep Time: 45 minutes
Cook Time: 1 hour
Serves: 8-10 people

Ingredients

For the Meat Sauce:
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 lb Italian sausage
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 (24 oz) jars marinara sauce
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
For the Cheese Mixture:
  • 15 oz ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1/2 tsp salt
For Assembly:
  • 12 lasagna noodles, cooked
  • 4 cups mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • 1 cup Parmesan cheese, grated

Instructions

  1. Make meat sauce: Brown beef and sausage in a large pot. Add onion and garlic, cook until soft. Stir in marinara, tomato paste, and seasonings. Simmer 30 minutes.
  2. Prepare cheese mixture: Mix ricotta, egg, parsley, and salt in a bowl.
  3. Cook noodles: Boil lasagna noodles according to package directions. Drain and set aside.
  4. Assemble lasagna: In a greased 9x13 dish, layer:
    • 1/3 meat sauce
    • 4 noodles
    • 1/2 ricotta mixture
    • 1/3 mozzarella
    • Repeat layers, ending with noodles, remaining sauce, and cheeses
  5. Bake: Cover with foil and bake at 375Â°F for 45 minutes. Remove foil and bake 15 more minutes until bubbly.
  6. Rest: Let it cool for 15 minutes before serving
